Paddy Seperator

Paddy Separator Working Principle

Paddy Separator Machine is used to separate the brown rice from the paddy that is not be husked, it workds according to the difference of specific gravity, granularity and friction coefficient between paddy and brown rice, through the special separating tray to screen the mixture continuously, making it in stratification state. Due to the function of separation, big-density and small-size brown rice towards to the upper of the separating tray, Small specific gravity and large-size paddy towards to the lower part of the separating tray, then separating pure brown rice, mixture and pure paddy.

DKTL Rice Hull Separator

Separating the paddy and the broken brown rice from husk

Rice Hull Separator is used for separating the paddy and the broken brown rice from husk. The half-baked grain, shrunken rice and so on. Extracted secondary grain may be used as raw material of feedstuff and brewing. The Rice Hull Separator usually installed behind the rice hulling machine and in the front of the rice husk wind tunnel. The rice husk separated by the separator is collected by the brake dragon. The fine dust of exhaust gas sent by the fan can be re-collected by bag filter, pulse or other forms of dust collector to ensure that emissions are in line with environmental requirements.
